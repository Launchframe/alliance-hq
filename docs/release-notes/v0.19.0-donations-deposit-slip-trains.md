## Summary

**Donations**

- **Commander store gifts** — Officers log gold-brick peer gifts with receipts; alliance and personal prize-spend ledgers; shareable tip-jar badges at `/b/{code}`.

**Banks & deposit slips**

- **Bank context from video** — Deposit-slip uploads detect bank-info frames to match or create banks; new bank-lifecycle guide at `/guides/bank-lifecycle`.
- **City List import** — See which rows are New vs already In HQ; optionally soft-archive banks missing from a complete import.
- **Deposit-slip review** — Softer submit gates, live flagged-cluster panel, Follow Me + jump-to-issue, game timestamps, alliance tag auto-correct, and AppSelect typeahead on term pickers.
- **OCR eval** — Shadow fingerprint pass compares native OCR against submitted scores for admin analytics.

**Trains**

- **VS upload funnel** — Banner nudges officers to upload yesterday's VS scores before spinning; dashboard refreshes when you return from upload.
- **Week-strip paint** — Long-press a day on the week strip to paint a template without opening month view.
- **Top VS/VR scopes** — Configurable Top N for VS and VR conductor wheels with asymmetric VR reporter gates.
- **Prior-day VS scores** — VS Top 1/10 wheels use yesterday's Ashed VSScore, not season HQ VR.
- **Light mode** — Readable mechanism labels on calendar tiles in light theme.

**THP, video & Discord**

- **THP Power Details** — Geometry-first OCR for more reliable 12-digit power parsing.
- **Live video progress** — SSE listen clients use the unpooled DB URL so progress bars update without reload.
- **Video review** — Errors surface next to Save; roster power parsing handles K/M/B without clobbering level/profession.
- **Discord channels** — R4/R5 channel-setter gate applies consistently across banking, VR, events, profession, and train commands.

## Breaking changes

- None for operators. Donation pooling (Venmo/PayPal/etc.) is intentionally deferred.
