## Summary

Video handoff gets cross-device uploads, admin filters, OCR accuracy badges, and survey preview autoplay — with roster OCR hardened for local and serverless deploys. VR adds institute-level ladders and weekly pass boost; trains add The Price Is Right economy and a provisional week schedule preview. Commander claim flows improve via join-code claim, Discord inheritance after `/link`, roster-link inbox fixes, and member-link help redirect. Auth adds sign-in quick access and connect return-path; the HQ shell consolidates settings, unifies the members roster, and trims nav. Observability adds Speed Insights and DB health events; LISTEN/SSE paths tolerate Neon credential rotation.
